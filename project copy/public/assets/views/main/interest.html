Softball

I have been playing softball since I was a kid, and it has been a constant source of passion, discipline, and growth in my life. Today, I am proud to play collegiate softball at Fordham University, where I compete as a catcher. The game has shaped who I am—on and off the field—teaching me the value of teamwork, resilience, and leadership.

Concerts

Live music is one of my absolute favorite things—I have been to over 30 concerts and counting! My very first show was a Bob Marley cover band, and I’ve been hooked on the energy of live performances ever since. From small venues to packed crowds, there is something electric about being surrounded by people who all love the same music. I listen to just about everything, but I am especially drawn to indie punk, pop, and rock. Some of the most unforgettable shows I have been to include Peter McPoland, Conan Gray, Declan McKenna, and most recently, Vundabar. Each concert is a memory I carry with me—and I am always looking forward to the next one.

Theatre and Musicals

There’s something magical about live theatre that never fails to captivate me. I’m completely obsessed with musicals—the storytelling, the music, the emotion— it all pulls me in. I’ve seen my favorite show, Hadestown, four times and could honestly go four more! I’ve been lucky enough to see incredible productions on Broadway like Cabaret, Sweeney Todd, The Great Gatsby, and Chicago, as well as touring performances of Hamilton and Mean Girls. Beyond just enjoying the shows, I love digging into the storytelling—analyzing the lyrics, themes, and characters long after the curtain closes. Some of my favorite performers are Eva Noblezada and Jeremy Jordan; their voices and stage presence are absolutely unforgettable. Theatre will always have my heart (and a spot in my calendar for the next show).

Reading

Reading has always been a huge part of my life—there’s nothing like getting lost in a good story. I’m especially drawn to fantasy and dystopian novels that let me escape into new worlds and explore big ideas through imaginative storytelling. I’m currently building my own personal library, which has grown to over 100 books (and counting!). One of my most prized finds is a book from 1929, which sits proudly on my shelf. Right now, I’m making my way through the Harry Potter series, with plans to reread The Hunger Games next—especially with Sunrise on the Reaping recently coming out. After that, I’m diving headfirst into the sprawling world of Sarah J. Maas. My TBR list is never-ending, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

Baking

Baking has always been one of my favorite creative outlets—there’s something so rewarding about turning simple ingredients into something delicious. I’ve experimented with everything from cakes and breads to more intricate pastries, but my specialty is Oreo cupcakes. In high school, I even competed in a Cupcake Wars fundraiser where I baked and sold over 500 cupcakes! Whether I’m testing a new recipe or making a batch of my go-to treats for friends and family, baking is a sweet way for me to relax, share joy, and get a little messy in the kitchen.
